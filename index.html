BJJ Data

This website is an open source collection of 24 YouTube clips of submissions, performed by world champions during tournaments. The idea is to make it easier to analyze Brazilian jiu-jitsu details that have been proven to work. Click the filters below to narrow down the clips you'd like to see.

Contribute clips on Github. Data processing date: 2023-01-29T20:24:30.807Z. Fetch the JSON data to embed it on your site.
